Destinations

I've been drawn to Iceland because of its stunning natural beauty. The landscape is nothing
short of mystical. The scenary promises an endless series of snow-covered volcanoes, mountains
and ice fields. A phenonem that must be seen in person once in their lifetime is the clash of
the volcano's with the ice cold water, the effect created is magical even in video form. With a
rugged, “other-worldly” terrain, the Nordic icy views of Iceland are just captivating. The rest of my
trip would be India. One incetive being to experience my trip with my family. However, the reason
being that every state has a unique culture and identity. Combined with the interconnected train
network concludes my ideal trip.

qualities in person. Third would be seeing penguins in Antartica.
